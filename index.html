STARTUP SCRAMBLE

TUESDAY, OCT 28, 2014

An inspirational evening of art, music, talks, and socializing with the hopes of generating more citizen entrepreneurs while highlighting the accomplishments of our existing community members.

  • Learn from experienced entrepreneurs. Attending will provide you tools to help turn your idea into a business.

  • Experience the art, music, and food, that make Buffalo famous. We're taking over Allen St. from Symphony Circle to Elmwood Ave.

  • The best part of startup culture is the people. Introduce yourself to the movers and shakers - you might meet your co-founder.

Event Overview

Starting at 5:30 pm, Startup Grind will be hosting Stephen Kiernan II at DBGBs to kickoff the evening. Bringing dozens of community leaders, organizers, and entrepreneurs to the area.

From 5pm - 10pm venues along Allen St. will host different community groups and companies. These participating organizations will provide entertaining and educational programming to attract people to the venues. Artists and musicians will create a festival atmosphere throughout Allentown to compliment the educational components.

43North Week

Startup Scramble is part of 43North Week, a six-day celebration of innovation and entrepreneurship in Buffalo, N.Y. from Oct. 24 to Oct. 30, 2014 that builds up to the intense finals of the world's largest business idea competition. Learn more at 43north.org/week.

Keynote Speaker

Stephen Kiernan II

Stephen Kiernan II is an entrepreneur, executive and investor. He is the President of Algonquin Studios, a company from the first-wave of information technology startups in Buffalo, NY. After 16 years in business and successfully spinning off 8 companies, the founders of Algonquin Studios are starting Buffalo's first Tech Startup Accellerator VCAMP. VCAMP stands for Validate, Challenge, Accelerate, Mentor, Promote, it's designed to take early stage startups from idea to revenue in under twelve months, using Lean Startup methodologies. Limited Seating is available, reserve your place today at the Startup Grind Buffalo website!
