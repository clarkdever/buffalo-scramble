The last week in October will host a series of events focused on the emerging Startup economy in Buffalo.

Buffalo’s 4th Startup Weekend will be held at D!G on October, 24-26, The Bright Forum Wednesday, October 29th will have the semi-finalists from 43North’s business plan competition presenting to investors.

October 30th, will be the marquee event when 43North will announce which 11 new companies will receive $5 million in funding in exchange for starting their business in Buffalo. All three of these planned events will create an impactful “Startup Week” in Buffalo - but none of them offer a chance to engage the majority of residents in celebrating the revitalization of our local economy.

This is also a way to create a lasting first impression on 43North Participants and Sponsors that may be visiting Buffalo for the first time. To address these needs and inspire more residents to help bootstrap the economy through the creation of small businesses and high growth startups, we are proposing: Startup Scramble..

    Rohini Srihari is an entrepreneur, scientist and educator. She has been involved with language technology companies for over a decade. She has worked extensively with the US Government, specifically the Department of Defense in developing innovative multilingual text mining solutions. She has given numerous talks and published extensively in the areas of text mining and multilingual text analysis.
